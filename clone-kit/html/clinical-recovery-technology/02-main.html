Leading-edge clinical technology

Pushing the boundaries of clinical excellence

At Paracelsus Recovery, we use the most advanced, state-of-the-art medical technologies to ensure unparalleled assessment, diagnostics and treatment precision.  From 24-hour ECGs, IV infusions, and HRV testing to stress MRIs and Intermittent Hyperoxic Hypoxic treatment, our suite of tools represents the forefront of medical innovation.

Not all of these technologies are suitable for every client. Some are used by default, while others, especially more invasive diagnostic tools, are only employed when indicated and always with the client’s consent.

Unlike traditional ECGs offering a two-dimensional snapshot, the 3D ECG captures a more detailed and comprehensive picture of heart function. This precision allows us to detect irregularities such as arrhythmias, ischaemia, or structural abnormalities that might otherwise go unnoticed. A healthy heart is crucial for both optimal physical well-being and emotional regulation.

Unlike a standard ECG, which offers a brief snapshot, this test provides a complete picture of how your heart performs during various activities, rest periods, and sleep. By understanding the interaction between stress, activity, and heart function, this test helps us understand what physical health issues you are struggling with, which we will then address in your treatment programme.

Heart Rate Variability (HRV) measures the variation in time between heartbeats, offering insights into your autonomic nervous system’s balance and stress levels. Conversely, carotid ultrasound evaluates the health of your carotid arteries, which supply blood to the brain, detecting plaque build-up or narrowing that could lead to strokes.

We use HRV testing & Carotid Ultrasounds to assess your body’s ability to recover from stress and maintain equilibrium. In doing so, we gain a comprehensive understanding of your cardiovascular health. Mental health issues take a great toll on our hearts and our ability to cope with stress. If we can amend these imbalances, it protects you against physical risks and promotes mental clarity and emotional stability.

Stress echocardiography combines ultrasound imaging of the heart with stress tests to assess how the heart functions under physical or emotional stress. This test detects heart conditions that may not be apparent during rest. We use stress echocardiography to identify cardiovascular risks linked to stress. By addressing these issues, we support heart health and mental well-being, helping you manage stress effectively and confidently.

A full-body MRI provides a comprehensive scan of the body, offering high-resolution imaging of organs, tissues, and structures. This non-invasive diagnostic tool detects abnormalities, inflammation, and potential health concerns at an early stage. At Paracelsus Recovery, we use full-body MRI scans as part of our holistic diagnostic process to identify underlying issues that may affect your physical and mental health. By providing a complete overview of your internal health, this technology helps create highly personalised treatment plans, ensuring optimal recovery and well-being while offering peace of mind.

The Biobeat system continuously monitors vital signs such as heart rate, oxygen saturation, respiratory rate, and blood pressure for 24 hours. This innovative wearable technology provides real-time data, making it ideal for managing detoxification processes or monitoring high-risk conditions.

The Aktiia system provides continuous, non-invasive blood pressure monitoring over 24 hours. Unlike traditional cuff-based methods, this technology allows for seamless and accurate tracking, capturing daily and night fluctuations.

We use 24-hour blood pressure monitoring to understand how factors like stress, activity levels, and sleep impact your cardiovascular system and, thus, by extension, your mental and physical well-being

The OXA Breathing Coach is an advanced device designed to guide and optimise your breathing patterns. Measuring respiratory rate, heart rate, and other metrics provides feedback to help you develop deep, controlled breathing techniques.

At Paracelsus Recovery, we use the OXA device as part of our stress management and relaxation therapies. Controlled breathing improves oxygen delivery to your organs and activates the parasympathetic nervous system, reducing stress and promoting mental calmness.

Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS) uses gentle electrical pulses to stimulate the vagus nerve, which plays a key role in regulating the body’s stress response, digestion, and overall inflammation. This therapy enhances the parasympathetic nervous system, promoting relaxation and reducing physical tension.

Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) tracks blood sugar levels in real-time, offering critical insights into metabolic health. This technology detects fluctuations caused by diet, stress, or hormonal imbalances, allowing for precise interventions.

Without adequate sleep, our physical and mental health will plummet. Sleepiz is a non-invasive device that monitors sleep patterns and detects issues like sleep apnoea, snoring, and other disruptions. We use Sleepiz to diagnose and address sleep-related issues, enhancing restorative sleep. By improving your sleep quality, we can guarantee mental health benefits like a boost in energy, cognitive performance, and emotional resilience, on top of the countless physical health benefits that come with improved sleep.

Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis (BIA) measures body composition, including fat mass, muscle mass, and hydration levels. This detailed scan helps assess overall physical health and identify imbalances that may affect energy or metabolism. We use BIA to guide personalised nutrition and fitness plans.

NAD and Orthomolecular IV infusions deliver essential nutrients, vitamins, and coenzymes like NAD directly into the bloodstream. This therapy enhances cellular repair, reduces inflammation, and boosts energy production. At Paracelsus Recovery, we use these infusions to ensure to rejuvenate your body on a cellular level and ensure that your internal systems are in balance.  

The Whoop Band and Oura Ring are advanced wearable devices that track sleep, recovery, and activity levels, providing real-time data on your body’s performance and readiness. These devices offer insights into stress levels, recovery needs, and overall physical condition.

IHHT alternates between high and low oxygen levels during therapy, training your cells to become more efficient at oxygen use. This therapy boosts mitochondrial health, detoxifies the body, and enhances overall resilience. By enhancing cellular efficiency, this therapy provides a sense of renewed energy and clarity, strengthening both physical and emotional well-being.

Biofeedback devices like Bio-resonance and Metatron analyse the body’s electromagnetic fields to identify imbalances and provide corrective feedback. These non-invasive tools support energy alignment and holistic healing.

The Satori Chair aligns your brain wave patterns through combining sound and vibration therapy. In so doing, it changes your overall brainwave frequency and induces a deeply relaxing and meditative experience. Stimulating the senses encourages stress reduction and emotional release. We use the Satori Chair to help manage anxiety, improve focus, and achieve a state of calm.

Photobiomodulation uses red and near-infrared light to stimulate cellular repair and regeneration. This therapy reduces inflammation, boosts circulation, and supports overall physical recovery. We use photobiomodulation to enhance healing, reduce pain, and improve skin health. The therapy’s calming effects also promote mental clarity and reduce stress, contributing to overall well-being.

Neurofeedback is a cutting-edge therapy that trains your brain to regulate its own activity. By using real-time monitoring of brainwaves, neurofeedback helps improve mental function, emotional regulation, and cognitive performance. This non-invasive treatment can reduce symptoms of anxiety, depression, ADHD, and stress by retraining the brain to create more balanced and efficient neural patterns. It enhances focus, emotional stability, and overall mental clarity, contributing to both psychological well-being and improved quality of life.

AI-powered psychiatry uses advanced artificial intelligence algorithms to assist us in diagnosing, monitoring, and personalising your mental health treatments. By analysing vast amounts of data, including medical histories, behavioural patterns, and symptom tracking, AI can help us develop more accurate and tailored treatment plans. AI-powered psychiatry enhances the precision and efficiency of our diagnostic process.
