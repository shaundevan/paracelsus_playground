Your Programme

The foundation for a longer, happier life

Many of the conditions that shorten our lives are completely preventable if identified early enough. Our longevity analysis and assessment provide you with that life-extending information. During 7 days, every possible health risk will be investigated by specialist doctors from every field.

Once diagnostics are complete, you’ll receive a comprehensive health overview with personalised recommendations for further treatment and lifestyle adjustments. In just seven days, you’ll learn how to minimize your risk of developing the five biggest killers and discover how to add high-quality years to your life.

A World Leading Programme

Most of the conditions that shorten our lives are preventable when caught early. Our goal is to provide you with the knowledge to identify these risks before they become serious.

With our support, you’ll have the opportunity to make informed adjustments to your lifestyle and environment, reducing the chances of developing conditions like heart disease, cancer, respiratory issues, stroke, and Alzheimer’s, which are the leading causes of death in the western world according to the World Health Organisation.

Four to six weeks after your stay, you will receive your assessment report and have a full debrief in person or online. A 100-page list of recommendations can seem daunting, but our team will make this process as easy as possible for you. We provide a step-by-step implementation and management process for all measures that will improve your longevity and quality of life.

Our health assessment includes:

  • Full Medical Check-Up: Comprehensive health tests on day one, including medical history, blood work, gut biology, stress hormones, mitochondrial function, and a full-body MRI.
  • Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS): A once-in-a-lifetime DNA analysis revealing unique genetic insights, identifying potential health risks, and shaping tailored health recommendations.
  • Cardiovascular Health: In-depth heart and stress analysis through 24-hour ECG, heart rate variability, and ultrasound.
  • Neurological Health: Cognitive function, memory, and Alzheimer’s risk assessment using MRI, genetic testing, and EEG.
  • Psychiatric Well-being: Mental health diagnosis by our psychiatrist to address conditions affecting overall wellness.
  • Respiratory Function: Lung function tests alongside lifestyle and environmental assessments.
  • Cancer Screenings: Comprehensive blood and imaging tests, including optional endoscopies.
  • Sleep Studies & Specialised Exams: Personalised sleep monitoring, dermatology, gynaecology, dental checks, and more.

That’s why we go far beyond standard health checks to include a comprehensive assessment of your mental health, sleep quality, and stress levels.
