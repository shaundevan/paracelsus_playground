Your Programme

No patient is ever the same,  so no treatment plan is either. 

We can’t tell you exactly what your four-week stay will involve because our patients have no pre-defined treatment path. Everyone who comes to stay at Paracelsus Recovery receives a bespoke treatment plan, with each day thoughtfully structured to include a range of carefully tailored therapeutic elements and treatments.

These therapies are designed to complement one another, ensuring that every aspect of your physical, emotional, and psychological health is addressed. See below for more details.

Tailored to you from the very start

We conduct an elaborate assessment procedure at the beginning of your programme, with tests ranging from extensive medical check-ups and laboratory tests to psychiatric and functional medicine assessments.

Based on the results of these assessments, our lead doctors create a detailed treatment plan in collaboration with the rest of the multidisciplinary team that is 100% tailored to your individual situation and needs.

Defining success in mental health and addiction treatment isn’t straightforward because it looks different for everyone. For one person, success might mean complete sobriety, while for another, it could mean drinking significantly less and feeling more in control. In mental health, it could be the difference between daily panic attacks and just one a year, or simply feeling more stable and resilient in everyday life.

At Paracelsus Recovery, we focus on what success means for you. Rather than relying on generic statistics, we prioritise lasting, meaningful change; helping you build a healthier, more fulfilling life in a way that feels right for you. Through personalised care and ongoing support, we work to ensure that the improvements you make during treatment continue long after you leave.
