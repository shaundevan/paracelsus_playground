unrivalled service

A dedicated team, just for you

Connection is often cited as the motor behind change, growth and healing. Not only will you work with 15+ leading medical experts, mental health professionals and complementary therapists, but you will also have a 5-person hospitality team focused exclusively on you and your needs.  

Our in-house housekeeping team will ensure that your penthouse apartment is thoroughly cleaned daily. If you would prefer a cleaning service twice daily or every two days, then we will do that.

A private driver and a recent-model Bentley Flying Spur from our fleet will be assigned to you during your stay with us. They are available around the clock to bring you wherever you might need or like to go.  

A private chef will create gourmet dining experiences for each of your daily meals. Highly trained in both gastronomy and psychonutrition, your chef will work closely with your nutrition team to ensure that each meal is exquisitely prepared, tailored to your unique nutritional needs, and aligned with your vitamin and mineral requirements. Every dish also takes into account any intolerances or specific medical conditions, ensuring both nourishment and indulgence.

Our client-relations team is there to help you organise trips, plan visits from loved ones, organise additional treatments or activities, manage work-related travel, and whatever other tasks you might need assistance with during your stay with us. 

support when you need it most

Your wellness is our priority

Recovery is a journey and rarely an easy one. Making sure you feel as at ease as possible is crucial when it comes to restoring your health and facilitating your healing.

The Dolder Grand, our partner hotel, is heralded as one of the most renowned hotels in Europe and has repeatedly been awarded the prestigious ‘Best Spa in Europe’ award. Access to the Dolder Grand Spa is included in our treatment programmes, which is only a few minutes away from our clinic. 

We understand the importance of family and professional commitments. We provide tailored support to help you maintain vital connections with loved ones while focusing on your recovery. Family members are encouraged to be as involved in your recovery as you see fit. We also provide family therapy or family psychoeducational sessions.

We can adapt your treatment programme to suit your work schedule. For instance, if you need to travel during your stay with us, we can organise for your team to fly with you. Private office spaces and secure communication options are also available.

The mind-body connection lies at the heart of our holistic approach to health, longevity and well-being. From daily massages and yoga practices to acupuncture, sound healing and breathwork, your treatment programme will include a number of wellness treatments that we believe will help you on your recovery journey.
