Your Programme

The Integration Stage

When you are in treatment, all of your energy is focused on restoring your health, whether that’s focusing on managing withdrawal symptoms or processing difficult emotions. However, once you finish treatment, many of the triggers, stressors, and challenging relationship dynamics that heightened your difficulties can reoccur upon returning home.

Our aftercare team offers flexible support that helps you manage these difficulties and integrate what you learnt during treatment into your life. We’re here to ensure your recovery is sustainable and convenient, with options for as many virtual sessions or in-person visits as needed.

We are always here for you

At Paracelsus Recovery, your well-being is our top priority, not just during treatment but long after. We understand that recovery is a lifelong journey, and we are committed to being here for you every step of the way. Our comprehensive aftercare programme is designed to provide you with the support, guidance, and resources needed to maintain long-term recovery.

Our aftercare programme is individually tailored and designed to flexibly adapt to your evolving needs. Typically, it includes your live-in therapist accompanying you home for several weeks or months to continue providing support. Alongside this, you can have ongoing online sessions with your lead psychiatrist, ensuring consistent guidance and adjustments to your treatment plan. This may involve remote management of your supplementation and meal plans, with updates based on your progress. We also encourage re-testing of certain lab values, which can be coordinated with your GP or through a return visit to us. Additionally, follow-up check-ins can be arranged to help consolidate your recovery, providing continued support a few months after completing the intensive treatment phase.

our approach

Ongoing support tailored to you

Your aftercare programme will be tailored to your unique needs and preferences, drawing on our clinicians’ decades of experience to ensure the most sustainable outcomes. We carefully consider which elements of your treatment are essential for long-term success and continue to support you in maintaining progress.

Depending on those specific needs, aftercare can be provided for a few weeks up to a few months. Ongoing support will be tailored to your exact circumstances but often includes ongoing therapy, family and couple therapy, remote or in-person psychiatric support, ongoing management of nutritional supplements and, most importantly, ongoing comprehensive health management, with a focus on continuous and sustainable recovery beyond your time in residential rehab.

treatments & therapies



Client centred care

Our aftercare services are centred on the therapeutic alliance you form with your team during your time at our residential rehab. To that end, your live-in therapist can also travel home with you to help you integrate what you’ve learnt in treatment into your real-world life and relationships. In so doing, they can continue to help you manage everything from cravings and relationship difficulties to work stress or any other problems that may arise upon returning home.

Your health and happiness matter to us, and we’re here to ensure you feel supported and cared for every day, no matter where life takes you.
