OUR APPROACH

The most comprehensive and innovative treatment worldwide 

A proven method

At Paracelsus Recovery, we recognise that our health is a product of our relationship with ourselves. That is why we do not adopt a ‘one-size-fits-all’ approach. Instead, we adapt each programme to each client’s unique set of needs. Our approach uses cutting-edge treatments combined with holistic care and guidance from our team of world-renowned doctors, therapists, and specialists to inform and support you on your journey to recovery.

A 360-degree treatment that considers the whole person: mind, body, and soul.

Paracelsus Recovery was the first treatment centre to introduce functional medicine and interdisciplinary methods into its treatment programme. Since the beginning, we’ve known that symptoms are a consequence of imbalances in life. Healing requires understanding how and why they came to be so that we can treat the underlying cause, not just the symptoms.

Assessments+

Our path to healing, tailored entirely to you.

Each of our treatment programmes is carefully curated to each client’s unique set of needs. Those needs may be physical and psychological, e.g. if our team identifies nutritional deficiencies or signs of depression during the assessment period, your programme would be shaped with these in mind. Our pragmatic nature extends into the every day; from work to travel obligations, we are happy to accommodate client needs.

Cutting edge medicine

We work closely with the Zurich University Clinic, ensuring we remain at the forefront of medical research. Many of our key team members have played pivotal roles in shaping the present, and continue to shape the future, of their respective disciplines.

Your programme will be curated by world-renowned doctors, including a Nobel Prize laureate and Harvard Medical School alumni. Cutting-edge advancements in medical care guide our work; whether it’s a full-body MRI, one of our 70+ laboratory tests, or psychedelic psychotherapy and vitamin infusions, we will make sure to provide each diagnostic and treatment necessary for your recovery.

diagnostics & Technologies+

We meet you where you are at

For over 12 years, we have treated all kinds of health conditions. Often, our clients find themselves alienated, deeply dependent on something that’s harming their health, and feeling shame or paranoia. At Paracelsus Recovery, we strive for growth, whether that’s working with your team or solely with you, or treating you for weeks or just hours at a time. We take the steps to ease you into your journey to healing. 

For instance, if you’re struggling with substance abuse, growth might mean total sobriety or it might mean using less. If it’s a mental health issue, it might mean completely eradicating the condition from your life, or it might involve finding ways to make your symptoms work for you, rather than against you. 

Alongside tailoring our programme to your specific needs, we guarantee complete confidentiality throughout your time with us. So, whatever growth means for you, we are here to help you achieve it.

A place of healing

Switzerland is synonymous with medical excellence, and for good reason. Health and well-being are ingrained into our way of life; a product of the safety, nature, and quality of care we grow up with.

We are based in Zurich – a city with some of the world’s best hospitals, clinics, and medical facilities, many of which are our partner hospitals and centres. The city’s blend of modern medicine, clean air, and world-renowned landscapes makes it an unparalleled place for growth, clarity, and starting over.
